### Q12. A new IT start-up entering a mature, dominated market should primarily focus on:

- **A)** Matching incumbents on price immediately
- **B)** Identifying a differentiated niche and building a compelling value proposition
- **C)** Replicating the largest player's business model
- **D)** Targeting the same client segments as market leaders

<details>
<summary>✅ Answer & Explanation</summary>

**Correct Answer: B)** Identifying a differentiated niche and building a compelling value proposition

**Explanation:** New entrants must find a differentiated niche — competing head-on with incumbents is a recipe for failure.

**Difficulty:** Easy

</details>
