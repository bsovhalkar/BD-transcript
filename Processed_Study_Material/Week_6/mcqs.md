### Q16. The correct sequence for strategic marketing (STP framework) is:

- **A)** Segment, Target, Position, Execute
- **B)** Position, Segment, Target, Execute
- **C)** Target, Segment, Execute, Position
- **D)** Execute, Target, Position, Segment

<details>
<summary>✅ Answer & Explanation</summary>

**Correct Answer: A)** Segment, Target, Position, Execute

**Explanation:** The STP framework: Segment the market, Target the right segments, Position the product — then Execute.

**Difficulty:** Easy

</details>
