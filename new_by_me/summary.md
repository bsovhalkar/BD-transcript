### 1. Which of the following is NOT listed as a 'Cultural Blind' that blocks effective cross-cultural interaction?

- **A)** Extreme individualism
- **B)** Stereotyping

- **D)** Cultural synergy

> ✅ **Answer: D) Cultural synergy**

### 2. In the modern inverted pyramid model of a customer-centric organization, who is at the top?

- **A)** Top management
- **B)** Middle management
- **C)** Frontline staff
- **D)** Customers

> ✅ **Answer: D) Customers**
> 🔁 *Appeared 2 times | Week 1 | Lecture_W1L22_Markets_and_Marketing*

### 3. Which of the following is NOT one of the 10 factors of business development introduced in the lecture?

- **A)** Customers

- **C)** Human Resources
- **D)** Ownership

> ✅ **Answer: C) Human Resources**
> 🔁 *Appeared 2 times | Week 1 | Lecture_W1L24_Business_Development_Cases*

### 4. In the R&D spectrum, which type of research has the objective of pure knowledge acquisition with a very long timeline?

- **A)** Applied Research
- **B)** Developmental Research
- **C)** Basic Research
- **D)** Product Research

> ✅ **Answer: C) Basic Research**
> 🔁 *Appeared 2 times | Week 10 | Lecture_W10L103_Functional_Competencies*

### 5. What is the 'Demand Cascade' in market analysis?

- **A)** The process of increasing demand through aggressive advertising.
- **B)** The process by which the total potential market is funneled down to actual sales through economic and preferential filters.
- **C)** The rapid growth of demand in an emerging market.
- **D)** The decline in demand due to market saturation.

> ✅ **Answer: B) The process by which the total potential market is funneled down to actual sales through economic and preferential filters.**
> 🔁 *Appeared 2 times | Week 2 | Lecture_W2L26_Industry_and_Market*

### 6. What is defined as a broad, long-term statement expressing where a company aspires to be and its overarching purpose?

- **A)** Mission
- **B)** Strategy
- **C)** Vision
- **D)** Execution

> ✅ **Answer: C) Vision**
> 🔁 *Appeared 2 times | Week 1 | Lecture_W1L23_Strategy_Formulation*

### 7. What is the ultimate goal of a principled, balanced negotiation, as described in the lecture?

- **A)** Victory for one party over the other.
- **B)** Reaching an agreement at any cost.
- **C)** An agreement that provides equitable and substantial value to all parties involved.
- **D)** Demanding concessions as a condition of the relationship.

> ✅ **Answer: C) An agreement that provides equitable and substantial value to all parties involved.**
> 🔁 *Appeared 2 times | Week 10 | Lecture_W10L104_Negotiating_Skills*

### 📋 General Exam Tips

1. **Master definitions first** — Most Easy questions test precise definitions
2. **Learn framework components** — Frameworks appear repeatedly (7 Domains, 4Ps, Porter's 5 Forces, etc.)
3. **Remember company examples** — Toyota=TPS, IKEA=cost leadership, Apple=innovation
4. **Week 1 is foundational** — 35%+ of mock exam questions reference Week 1 concepts
5. **Week 10 is heavily tested** — BD Competencies account for ~30% of mock exam questions
6. **Read all 4 options carefully** — Hard questions have 2 plausible-sounding options
7. **Assignment questions are direct hints** — They signal what the instructor considers most important
8. **Case study weeks (12)** — Focus on application of frameworks to real scenarios
