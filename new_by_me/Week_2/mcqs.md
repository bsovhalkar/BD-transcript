*🔴 Hard | Source: Lecture_W2L33_Case_Study_of_Business_Development_Excellence*

- **A)** Launching a hostile takeover bid against HUL.
- **B)** Cutting prices drastically to match HUL's offers.

### Q2. What is the 'Demand Cascade' in market analysis?
*🔴 Hard | Source: Lecture_W2L26_Industry_and_Market*

- **A)** The process of increasing demand through aggressive advertising.
- **B)** The process by which the total potential market is funneled down to actual sales through economic and preferential filters.

- **D)** The decline in demand due to market saturation.

> ✅ **Correct Answer: B) The process by which the total potential market is funneled down to actual sales through economic and preferential filters.**
