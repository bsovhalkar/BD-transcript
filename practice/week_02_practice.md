# Week 2

## 1. Short Notes
- Three pillars of business success: **Product Innovation, Operational Excellence, Customer Fulfilment**.
- BD overlay: Strategic Marketing + Firm-Customer Connect + Brand Equity + Customer Loyalty.
- Three approaches to BD: **Transactional, Opportunity, Strategic**.
- Methods of market development: Geographic expansion, new segments, new products, acquisitions.
- Physical vs. digital marketing; B2C vs. B2B marketing differ in process, branding, and promotion.
- **Keywords:** Brand equity, customer loyalty, product innovation, franchise, omnichannel, market development.
- **Associations:**
  - Product innovation → differentiation → premium pricing → brand equity.
  - Customer fulfilment → loyalty → repeat revenue.
  - Operational excellence → cost leadership → competitive margins.
