# Week 8

## 1. Short Notes
- Corporate brand equity = strong product portfolio + brand portfolio + financial performance + culture/ethics + ESG + stakeholder reputation.
- Happiest Minds (IT): Born Digital. Born Agile. — niche megatrend positioning from Day 1.
- Nirma (FMCG): disrupted detergent market with low-cost model; Karsanbhai Patel as founder-entrepreneur.
- Delhivery (logistics): B2B express logistics → D2C + B2C; platform-driven, tech-enabled.
- Zydus Wellness (nutraceuticals): Sugar Free → EverYuth → Complan; brand extension strategy.
- Airtel: digital-first transformation to fightback against Jio's entry; 5G readiness.
- Corporate development = building brand across core product, market niche, globalization, competitive growth ranking.
- **7 Ps of service marketing**: Product, Price, Place, Promotion, People, Process, Physical Evidence.
- **Keywords:** Corporate brand, ESG, 7Ps, digital transformation, brand extension, LAND-EXPAND-WIDEN, COE.
- **Associations:** Leader's brand equity → startup credibility → investor confidence → BD success.
