# Week 7

## 1. Short Notes
- Brand planning process: Diagnosis (product+market, situation, drivers, segmentation, mapping) → Prognosis (SWOT, CSFs, insights) → Objectives/Strategy/Execution.
- Positioning tools: Conjoint Analysis, Factor Analysis, Discriminant Analysis, MDS.
- **BrandAsset Valuator (BAV)**: Differentiation + Relevance (Brand Strength) | Esteem + Knowledge (Brand Stature).
- Four brand equity stages: **Identity → Meaning → Response → Resonance**.
- Brand equity quadrants: Rising Stars, Champions, Declining Icons, Laggards.
- **Servitization**: Moving from product to product-service-solution bundles (IoT + outcome-based contracts).
- Pricing dimensions: cost-plus, value-based, competitive, dynamic, geographic, yield management.
- **Three brand health metrics**: Market Share, Mind Share, Heart Share.
- Brand communication: Amul's topical advertising + consistent mascot = enduring equity.
- **Keywords:** Brand equity, positioning, BAV, servitization, pricing, PESTED, brand planning, market share.
- **Associations:** Mind/Heart share → leading indicators of future market share.
