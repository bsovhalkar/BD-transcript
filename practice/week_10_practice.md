# Week 10

## 1. Short Notes
- Primary Value Chain: R&D → Procurement/Inbound → Manufacturing → Outbound/Warehousing → Sales & Marketing → After-sales.
- Supportive value chains: Finance, HR, IT, Legal, Planning, Project Management + ESG.
- Outsourcing evolution: Captive → Make-or-Buy → Global Offshoring.
- **R&D types**: Basic (5–10 yrs, uncertain, grants) → Applied (3–5 yrs) → Development (1–3 yrs) → Manufacturing.
- Three operations strategy pillars: Manufacturing, Supply Chain, Digital Operating Model.
- **4 Ps of Marketing**: Product, Price, Place, Promotion.
- **Financial strategy**: ROCE, EBITDA, working capital management, capital allocation.
- **HR leadership**: Talent mapping, talent development, culture management = strategic BD levers.
- BD Competencies framework: Value Chain + Functional + Negotiating + Cultural + Leadership.
- **Keywords:** Primary value chain, ESG, outsourcing, offshoring, R&D types, ROCE, negotiation, cultural intelligence.
- **Associations:** Value chain mastery → effective BD positioning. R&D investment → innovation leadership.
