# Week 12

## 1. Short Notes
- Nine business transformation modes: Product Renewal, Manufacturing Renewal, Marketing Boost, Diversification, Digitalisation, Disruption, Business Turnaround, Handovers, Business Exits.
- Orchid Pharma: 100% EOU → Cephalosporin APIs → China market → API-first strategy builds high entry barriers.
- Hospira BD: Generic injectable pharma + market layer expansion = 33% revenue growth.
- **Three generic strategies** revisited: Cost Leadership, Differentiation, Focus — must be operationalized through value chain.
- **Titan Company BD**: From Tata + TN government JV to global watches + jewellery (Tanishq) + eyewear (Titan Eye+) + accessories.
- Business turbulence: Orchid's financial crisis shows growth without financial discipline = structural failure.
- Creating value: Customer value + Shareholder value + Stakeholder value = sustainable BD.
- **VUCA** environment shapes BD: Volatile, Uncertain, Complex, Ambiguous.
- Titan's BD phases: Startup (1984) → Growth (watches) → Diversification (Tanishq) → Transformation (digital, global).
- **Keywords:** EOU, Cephalosporin, Orchid Pharma, Titan, Tanishq, VUCA, transformation modes, creating value, turnaround.
- **Associations:** API choice → entry barriers. EOU structure → quality standards → Big Pharma access. Brand extension → adjacent market capture.
