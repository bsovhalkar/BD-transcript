# Week 4

## 1. Short Notes
- Industry vs. Market: One industry serves multiple markets; one market is served by multiple industries.
- **Seven Domains** (Mullins): Market Attractiveness, Target Segment Benefits, Industry Attractiveness, Sustainable Advantage, Team Missions, CSF Execution Ability, Value Chain Connectedness.
- **Six Key Market Influencers** (DESTNE): Demographic, Economic, Social-Cultural, Technological, Natural, Environmental (Regulatory).
- Market layers: **Potential → Available → Target → Secured**.
- Pyramid of Markets: **ToP** (high price, low volume) | **MoP** | **BoP** (low price, high volume, high power).
- Three marketing approaches: **Transaction** (per-sale focus) | **Opportunity** (market share focus) | **Strategic** (brand + ROI focus).
- PLC stages: Introduction → Growth → Maturity → Decline (S-curve).
- Structural market types: Government (Defence, PSU, Agencies) | Civilian (Private, Social Orgs, Individuals).
- **Keywords:** BoP, STP, PLC, market attractiveness, adjacencies, DESTNE, potential market, secured market.
- **Associations:** BoP → high volume → national economic power. Strategic marketing → sustainable competitive advantage.
