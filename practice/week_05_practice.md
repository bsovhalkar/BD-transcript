# Week 5

## 1. Short Notes
- Customer = buyer; Consumer = user. BD must understand both.
- Customer-oriented org: circular model where every function directly connects with customers.
- **DAWN Framework**: Desires, Aspirations, Wants, Needs — richer than Maslow.
- Maslow limitation: non-linear in practice due to demographic/socio-economic diversity.
- **DAMESTs**: Demographic, Socio-cultural, Economic, Lifestyle, Technological, Natural — customer characteristics.
- Vertical engagement = multiple products for one cluster; Horizontal = single product across many clusters.
- Customer value = (Usage + Ownership + Image + Resale) – (Purchase + Operating + Service + Image Loss Costs).
- Business customer ecosystem has 5 hierarchical needs from basic operations to sustainable operations.
- **Keywords:** DAWN, DAMESTs, customer value, vertical/horizontal engagement, consumer vs. customer, Maslow.
- **Associations:** DAWN > Maslow for marketing strategy. Horizontal scale first → then vertical scope.
