# Week 6

## 1. Short Notes
- **STP** (Segmentation → Targeting → Positioning) = foundation of market strategy.
- Four segmentation dimensions: **Geographic, Demographic, Psychographic, Behavioural**.
- **VALS** (1978): Values and Lifestyle Survey — two axes: primary motivation (ideals/achievement/self-expression) + consumer resources.
- Six customer personas: **Analytical, Experimental, Impulsive, Dynamic, Demanding, Loyal**.
- Behavioural influencers: word of mouth, herd behaviour, disruptive trends, waiting for next gen, situational, regulatory.
- Rural India: ~60% population; demand outpacing urban in certain FMCG quarters.
- **Awareness–Usage hierarchy**: Unaware → Aware → Tried → Repeated → Heavy User → Brand Loyal.
- Marketing funnel and digital segmentation (40+ apps per person).
- Export-oriented sectors: IT, Pharma, Apparel, Jewellery, Spices, CDMO.
- **Keywords:** STP, VALS, Descriptor, behavioural segmentation, psychographics, BoP, export-oriented.
- **Associations:** STP → brand loyalty. Descriptors → sharper market focus. VALS → psychographic segmentation.
