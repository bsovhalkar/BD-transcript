# Week 9

## 1. Short Notes
- Five BD structures: **Collaboration, Alliance, Joint Venture, Subsidiary, M&A** (from simplest to most complex).
- Coalitions = simplest; temporary, easy to assemble/dissolve (e.g., airline alliances, university exchange).
- Coalitions ≠ Cartels: Coalitions are transparent and customer-friendly; Cartels are secretive and anti-customer.
- **Franchising**: franchisor grants brand+territory rights; franchisee uses know-how for royalty.
- Technical Collaborations: know-how fee + running royalty; typically 5–10 years.
- Collaboration → Alliance → Financial Collaboration → JV → M&A (natural progression).
- **Four Innovation modes** (Pisano & Verganti): Innovation Plaza, Innovation Community, Chosen Circle, Consortium.
- Licensing quadrant: Few Products + Many Markets = optimal starting point (high return, manageable investment).
- **Keywords:** Coalition, cartel, franchisor, licensee, royalty, JV, M&A, know-how fee, co-share.
- **Associations:** Technical collaboration → technology transfer → reverse sourcing. Coalitions → trust → alliances.
